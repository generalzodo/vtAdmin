FARE CONDITONS  

1. The bus departs promptly as scheduled provided it is fully booked else the bus may be delayed to await more passengers to enable recovery of high fuel costs. Please endeavour to be at the bus station at least 15 mins before your bus departure time. Once the bus departs, it cannot be stopped for security reasons and to avoid causing inconveniences to passengers already onboard.


2. The bus stops at only the designated bus stations for security reasons.


3. You are entitled to a total luggage allowance of 10kg. Any luggage above 10kg will be charged at N100 per kg to a maximum of 25kg per bag subject to availability of boot space. If you choose to use our cargo service the luggage will be charged at N50 per kg. Oversized/irregular luggage can only be carried with the cargo service.


4. When you miss a bus (no show), we will issue you a new ticket at a discounted rate of ₦5,000. Fees to
reschedule your ticket before your bus departs: with more than 24 hrs to departure ₦3,000; with less than 24hrs to departure ₦4,000.


5. There is no refund after your ticket is booked. See full terms and conditions of carriage on our website.
