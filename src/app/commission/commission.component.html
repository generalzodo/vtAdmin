How Commission Rates Work

  • Commission rates are set per bus (by ID) and apply to all approved agents
  • The rate is a flat amount (in Naira) discount per passenger
  • For example, ₦300 commission means agents get ₦300 off per passenger booking
  • If booking multiple passengers, the discount is multiplied by the number of passengers
  • Commission rates are configured here, not during agent approval
